How to Play

🎵

Listen to the sound

You'll hear pink noise with one frequency boosted. Tap ▶ to hear it again (up to 3 replays — but fewer replays = more points!)

🎯

Identify the frequency

Tap the button that matches the boosted frequency. As you level up, the boost gets smaller and the choices get closer together.

⚡

Build your combo

Answer fast and consecutively to multiply your score. Hit 10 in a row for 5× FEVER MODE!

❤️

Don't run out of hearts

You start with 3 hearts. Wrong answers cost a heart. When hearts run out, the game ends. Watch an ad to get 1 more!
