Privacy Policy

Car Audio Tuner  ·  Last updated: March 2026

Short version: Car Audio Tuner does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Who we are

Car Audio Tuner is a free, open-source Progressive Web App for measuring and calibrating car audio systems. It is provided by Owen Overstreet and hosted at ooverstreet.github.io/Owen.

Microphone access

This app requests access to your device's microphone solely to perform real-time acoustic measurements of your car's audio system. Specifically:

  • The microphone audio stream is processed entirely on your device in real time using the Web Audio API.
  • No audio is ever recorded, saved, or uploaded.
  • No audio data leaves your device.
  • Microphone access is only active while you have tapped the "Start Measuring" button. You can revoke it at any time by tapping "Stop Measuring" or closing the app.

Data collection

Car Audio Tuner collects no personal data whatsoever. We do not use:

  • Analytics or tracking services
  • Advertising networks
  • Crash reporting services
  • Cookies
  • User accounts or sign-in
  • Backend servers of any kind

The app runs 100% client-side in your browser. No data is transmitted anywhere.

Local storage

The app saves one item to your device's local storage:

  • Theme preference (Night, Car, or Day) — so the app remembers your display preference between sessions.

This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

Audio output

The app can play test signals (pink noise, sine sweep) through your device's audio output. This audio is routed directly to your headphone jack or Bluetooth output. No audio content is stored or transmitted.

Third-party services

This app does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs. The only external resource loaded is the app itself, served via GitHub Pages (pages.github.com). Please refer to GitHub's Privacy Statement for information about how GitHub Pages serving works.

Children's privacy

This app does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to this policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date. Since no data is collected, such changes would only arise from structural changes to the app.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can open an issue on the GitHub repository.
