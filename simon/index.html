How to Play

👀

Watch the pattern

Buttons light up one at a time, each playing a unique frequency tone. Watch carefully — the sequence gets longer every round!

👆

Repeat it exactly

Tap the buttons in the same order. Get it right to advance. Miss a button and lose a life.

⚡

It gets faster

Every 5 rounds the pattern plays faster. At round 11 a 5th button unlocks. At round 21 a 6th button joins!

🎵

Use your ears

Each button plays a different frequency. Your brain will start to remember the sounds — not just the positions. That's ear training!
