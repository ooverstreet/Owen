How to use Car Audio Tuner

  • Connect your phone to your head unit via AUX / USB-C to 3.5mm cable
  • Select Pink Noise and adjust VOL so your car speakers are at listening volume
  • Place the phone at the driver's ear position with mic facing the speakers
  • Tap Start Measuring to activate the microphone
  • The orange dashed line is the Harman in-car target curve
  • The green line is your live measured response
  • The Delta grid shows how much to cut/boost each EQ band
  • Press Save to capture a before snapshot, adjust your EQ, and compare

Tip: enable Avg for a smoother display. Use 16-band if your head unit supports it.
