How to use Car Audio Tuner

  • Connect your phone to your head unit via AUX / USB-C to 3.5mm cable
  • Select Pink Noise and set VOL to a comfortable listening level
  • Place the phone at the driver's ear position, mic facing the speakers
  • Tap Start Measuring to activate the microphone
  • The orange dashed line is the Harman in-car target curve
  • The green line is your live measured response
  • The Delta grid shows dB to cut/boost each EQ band
  • Tap Save to capture a before snapshot, adjust EQ, and compare
  • Use the theme button to switch Night / Car / Day display

Tip: enable Avg for a smoother reading while making adjustments.
