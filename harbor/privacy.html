Privacy

Harbor · Last updated: July 2026

Short version: Harbor stores your account and posts so the community works. We don’t sell your data. Angel and Harbor Watch may send short text to an AI provider to reply or review flags. You can delete your account from Me.

Who we are

Harbor is a small coastal community app. The public site is at ooverstreet.github.io/Owen/harbor.

To reach the Harbor host, sign in and use Me → Contact the host. Your message is delivered privately — the host’s personal email is not shown publicly. If you don’t have an account yet, ask the friend who invited you.

What we store

  • Account details you provide (email, optional display name, optional profile photo)
  • Posts, replies, room/topic tags, and “I’ve felt this too” counts
  • Guidelines / Privacy & Terms acceptance timestamps
  • Moderation signals (reports, language strikes, admin actions) for safety
  • Activity stamps (such as last active) to protect quiet accounts
  • On-device preferences (theme, identity, invite unlock) in your browser

Where it lives

Cloud data is stored with Supabase (auth, database, and optional photo storage). The app itself is served by GitHub Pages.

AI features

Angel may send the text of a post (not your password) to an AI provider so it can leave a short companion note. Harbor Watch may review blocked-language samples for the host. If AI is unavailable, Harbor falls back to local wording. Do not put secrets or others’ private contact info in posts.

What we don’t do

  • We don’t sell personal data
  • We don’t run advertising networks inside Harbor
  • We don’t ask for your phone contacts

Your choices

  • Post anonymously or with a username
  • Skip a profile photo
  • Delete your account from Me (removes login and profile photo; posts may remain on the shore without your account attached)
  • Clear local cache on this phone from Me

Children

Harbor is meant for adults and older teens with care. It is not directed at children under 13. If you believe a child’s data was submitted, contact the host.

Changes

If this policy changes in a meaningful way, we’ll update the date above. Continued use after updates means you accept the revised policy.
